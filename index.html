Subzie

Subzie is a digital solution connecting sports players who want to play an organized league game with recreational sports teams who are short players for an upcoming game. Players are alerted of games that are in need of a substitute player based on predefined matching algorithms. Players then choose games from the matching games to their preferences and ‘Sub-In’ to join. Players who ‘Sub-In’ pay a drop-in fee based on the average cost of playing a game of that sport in the geographic region of the game. To reach a wide audience and hit the critical mass necessary to ensure games are always being filled, Subzie has partnered with multiple sports leagues who provide Subzie with their league database in return for potential new players trying out the league, insurance that games are not being canceled due to lack of players, and additional revenue from ‘Subs’.
